My Anime Recommendations

In this section, I've put together a collection of my all-time favorite anime that I think you should check out. These are the shows that have made a lasting impression on me. Some are classics, while others are modern hits that have taken the anime world by storm. Get ready for recommendations that cover a mix of genres, moods, and emotions!

Action

  • Jujutsu Kaisen - A perfect mix of action, suspense, and humor. Watch Trailer
  • My Hero Academia - A story of young heroes training to be the best in a world of superpowers. Watch Trailer
  • Attack on Titan - Humanity's fight for survival against giant monsters. Watch Trailer

Romance

  • Kaguya-sama: Love Is War - A hilarious, competitive romance between two student council members. Watch Trailer
  • Your Name - A beautiful, heartwarming tale of love and fate. Watch Trailer
  • Clannad: After Story - An emotional and tear-jerking romance with a deep story. Watch Trailer

Comedy

  • One Punch Man - A parody on superhero tropes with side-splitting humor. Watch Trailer
  • KonoSuba - A hilarious adventure with a group of quirky misfits in a fantasy world. Watch Trailer
  • The Devil is a Part-Timer! - Watch a demon lord work part-time at a fast-food restaurant! Watch Trailer

Horror

  • Tokyo Ghoul - A dark and thrilling story about a college student who becomes a half-ghoul. Watch Trailer
  • The Promised Neverland - A chilling tale of a group of orphans who discover the horrifying truth about the world outside their orphanage. Watch Trailer
  • Danganronpa - A suspenseful series set in a high school where students are trapped and forced to participate in a deadly game of murder. Watch Trailer

My Anime Recommendations Rating

Anime Title Genre My Rating Explanation of Rating
Attack on Titan Action A great anime with intense action and great story development. Loved the characters and world-building.
My Hero Academia Action An exciting show, but some parts feel a bit repetitive. Still, the character growth is fantastic.
Jujutsu Kaisen Action Incredible animation and fight scenes. The characters are fun, and the story is really engaging.
Kaguya-sama: Love is War Romance, Comedy Hilarious, well-written, and full of heart. It's a perfect blend of comedy and romance.
Your Name Romance Beautiful animation and a touching story about love and fate. A must-watch for romance lovers.
Clannad: After Story Romance A deeply emotional and powerful story that will leave you in tears.
One Punch Man Comedy Funny and fresh take on superhero tropes. A real joy to watch, even if it's a bit over the top.
KonoSuba Comedy Great characters and hilarious situations. A bit chaotic, but it works perfectly.
The Devil is a Part-Timer Comedy Funny premise with a nice balance of comedy and supernatural elements.
Tokyo Ghoul Horror Dark and gripping with a powerful psychological element, but can be very intense.
The Promised Neverland Horror Suspenseful and emotionally charged. One of the most exciting horror anime series.
Danganronpa Horror A thrilling, twisted mystery with a very unique premise.
